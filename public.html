About Us

A School Built for Excellence

Corona Model College is a premier private school located in the heart of Ibeju Lekki, Lagos State. We are committed to providing a world-class education that nurtures academic excellence, moral values and leadership skills in every student from Basic School through to Senior Secondary level.

Our dedicated faculty, modern facilities and structured curriculum ensure that every child reaches their full potential — academically, socially and personally.

What Makes Us Different

Structured Curriculum
